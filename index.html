Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a Seventh-day Adventist?

No! While the principles are based on Ellen G. White's writings, they're biblical truths that work for anyone who wants to manage money God's way.

How quickly will I see results?

Most people see immediate relief from tracking their spending. Debt elimination timelines vary, but with commitment, significant progress happens within 3-6 months.

What if I'm already deep in debt?

Perfect! That's exactly who these principles help most. We'll create a realistic plan based on your specific situation. No judgment, only solutions.

Is coaching done in person or online?

Both! Free workshops are in Ruacana. Group coaching is via Discord (online). Personal coaching can be in-person (Ruacana area) or video call.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I understand financial constraints. Contact me to discuss flexible payment options that work for your situation.

What if I can't afford coaching?

Start with the free workshop and resources. I also offer a limited number of scholarship spots each month for those in genuine need.
